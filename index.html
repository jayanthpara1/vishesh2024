About Vishesh 2k24

Malla Reddy Engineering College proudly presents Vishesh 2k24, our annual technical fest aimed at diversifying and exploring the manifold fields of technology. This two-day extravaganza brings together brilliant minds from across the country to showcase their talents, exchange ideas, and push the boundaries of innovation.

Vishesh 2k24 features a wide array of events including coding competitions, robotics challenges, technical paper presentations, and interactive workshops led by industry experts. With a focus on emerging technologies like AI, IoT, and sustainable engineering, we provide a platform for students to apply their theoretical knowledge to real-world problems.

Join us for an unforgettable experience of learning, competition, and networking as we celebrate the spirit of technological advancement and creativity. Vishesh 2k24 - where innovation meets inspiration!
